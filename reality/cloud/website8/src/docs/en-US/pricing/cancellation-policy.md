---
title: What is the cancellation policy?
priority: 1
---

8th Wall Pro plans and commercial licenses automatically renew every billing period until you cancel or schedule a commercial license end date in advance. You may cancel your 8th Wall plan at any time by logging into your 8th Wall account and going to the “Account” page. If you are canceling while on a paid plan, you will have access to 8th Wall through the end of your billing period. *There are no credits or refunds for partial or unused months.*

To schedule a commercial license end date in advance, please update the Project Duration settings for your Project ([instructions here](https://www.8thwall.com/docs/guides/projects/commercial-licenses-and-campaigns/#campaign-duration)).
When a commercial license ends, the project will be disabled  so it can no longer be viewed. This applies to both 8th Wall Hosted and Self-Hosted projects. This action cannot be undone.
