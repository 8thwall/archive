---
title: What is the cancellation and refund policy?
defaultpriority: 23
webarpriority: null
---

8th Wall subscriptions and licenses automatically renew every billing period until you cancel. You may cancel your 8th Wall plan at any time by logging into your 8th Wall account and going to the “Account” page. When you cancel while on a paid plan, you will have access to 8th Wall through the end of your billing period. There are no credits or refunds for partial or unused months.
