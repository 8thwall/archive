title: What is WebAR?
defaultpriority: 0
webarpriority: 1
---

WebAR, also known as web-based augmented reality, is the innovative technology that empowers augmented reality experiences directly within your web browser on any mobile device, including headsets. Unlike traditional AR applications, WebAR eliminates the need for users to download any additional apps. Users simply click on a link or scan a QR code to seamlessly open an AR-enabled web page right in their browser, ensuring a smooth and efficient augmented reality experience without the hassle of app downloads.
