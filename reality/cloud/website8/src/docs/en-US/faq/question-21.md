title: How much does 8th Wall cost?
priority: 21
---

8th Wall offers a free Basic plan, which gives you full access to our Niantic Studio product and its suite of tools to create projects and publish. For those creating commercial work for clients, a subscription is required. Our Pro subscription is $129 monthly and enables developers to purchase Commercial Licenses. Custom subscriptions are also available. More information can be found on our [Pricing Page](https://www.8thwall.com/pricing).
