title: What is the cost of Niantic VPS for Web?
defaultpriority: 25
---

Niantic VPS for Web offers a generous free tier, allowing developers to make up to 25,00 calls per month without any charges. Once a developer exceeds this limit, they will be billed starting from their 25,001st call in the first month. [See more here](https://8th.io/vps-pricing).
