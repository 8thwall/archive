---
title: What are the core features of 8th Wall AR Engine?
defaultpriority: 7
---

8th Wall's AR Engine features include:

- World Tracking: Create markerless experiences that allow the user to place interactive 3D content in their real world environment.
- Lightship VPS for Web:  Anchor WebAR content to real-world locations and access the 3D mesh of locations to enable occlusion, physics and a greater interactivity between virtual objects and the physical world.
- Image Targets: Allows web apps to instantly detect and track any uploaded image on flat and curved surfaces.
- Face Effects: Render face meshes or anchor 3D objects such as glasses or jewelry to a variety of face attachment points, including ears.
- Sky Effects: This technology separates the sky portion of a camera image from the rest of the scene. 8th Wall Sky Effects uses this to identify where the sky is. Once the sky is identified, developers can use Sky Effects to place digital content on top of it.
