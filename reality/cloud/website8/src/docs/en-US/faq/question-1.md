title: What is Augmented Reality?
defaultpriority: 0
webarpriority: 2
---

Augmented Reality (AR) is a technology which enables users to experience the real world together with digital information or objects at the same time. It requires a user to view the real world through a display, such as a mobile or wearable device, and makes use of computer vision technology to augment a user's experience of the world with computer graphics.
