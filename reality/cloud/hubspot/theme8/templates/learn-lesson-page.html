If you found your way to this lesson, you’re probably here because you’re interested in developing AR experiences. But as you started learning about in the video above, there’s more than one pathway to creating AR experiences.

As you continue to develop your interest in AR, you should explore these different options:

  • Web, where anyone can access the AR experience on their phones by scanning a QR code and/or going to a website
  • Native app, where anyone can access the AR experience but will need to download a new app
    • If you’re interested in developing native AR experiences, be sure to check out our sister product, [Niantic Lightship ARDK](https://lightship.dev/).
  • An existing app (usually a social network), where only users of that particular app can access your AR experience.
