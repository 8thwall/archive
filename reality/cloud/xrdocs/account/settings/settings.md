# Account Settings

The Account page allows you to:

* [Manage 8th Wall Credits](manage-credits)
* [View current subscriptions](view-subscriptions)
* [Manage Billing and Payment Methods](manage-billing)
* [Manage invoices](manage-invoices) (View and Download)
* [Set up Payments API](/legacy/guides/advanced-topics/monetize-with-8th-wall-payments/) to monetize your experiences or games with 8th Wall Payments.
* Access Platform API (**custom plans only**)
