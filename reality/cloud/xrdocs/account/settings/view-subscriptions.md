# View Subscriptions

1. Login to your 8th Wall account, and click "Go to My Projects":

![GoToMyProjects](/images/account/go-to-my-projects.jpg)

2. Expand your workspace, if not already open.

![SelectWorkspace](/images/account/select-workspace.jpg)

3. Click **Account** in the left navigation.

![SelectAccount](/images/account/select-account.jpg)

4. Click **Manage Billing and Payment Methods**

![ManageBilling](/images/account/manage-billing.jpg)

5. Current subscriptions (Credit and White Label) are displayed as the first section in the Stripe Billing portal. Each subscription will show renewal date and payment method. White Label subscriptions will also show the project they're associated with. To cancel a white label subscription, navigate to the [project dashboard](/account/projects/project-dashboard).

![CurrentSubscriptions](/images/account/current-subscriptions.jpg)
