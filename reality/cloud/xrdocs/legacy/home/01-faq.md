# Legacy FAQ

**For users on legacy Pro, Plus, or Starter Plans.**

For further questions or information, reach out to [licensing@8thwall.com](mailto:licensing@8thwall.com)

---

### If I am on a legacy Pro Plan, can I downgrade?
Yes, you can downgrade from your legacy Pro Plan to a free 8th Wall account. When you cancel your Pro Plan, you will lose access to the following features:

- Self-hosting and local development  
- The ability to use custom domains  
- Private publishing options for your projects  

You will not be able to subscribe to the Pro Plan again, as we are no longer offering it.

---

### If I downgrade my legacy Pro, Starter, or Plus plan to the free workspace, can I create new Cloud Editor projects?
Yes, all users on any account can access the Cloud Editor by clicking on **“Build with Legacy Tools”** from their workspace page.

---

### What happens to my existing 8th Wall Cloud Editor projects if I downgrade to the free workspace?
You will have access to all of your Cloud Editor projects even if you downgrade.

---

### What happens if I have an active Commercial License but I want to switch to a White Label subscription?
You will need to cancel your active Commercial License, then repurchase a White Label subscription through our **Purchasing Wizard** within your 8th Wall project.

---

### How does this pricing change impact my existing live commercial projects?
These changes do **not** impact any existing plan or commercial licenses. If you take no action, existing plans and commercial licenses will continue to automatically renew each billing period at current rates.

---

### Am I able to create custom domains with the free workspace?
No. The free 8th Wall Account does not support custom domains at this time.

---

### Can I self-host on the free 8th Wall Account?
No, you have to use 8th Wall hosting for all of your projects on the free workspace.

---

### If I do not purchase a White Label subscription, can I launch a commercial experience with the free workspace?
Yes, you can launch a commercial experience without a White Label subscription, but all projects are required to have a **Splash Screen with the 8th Wall logo** and a **“Powered by 8th Wall” logo** on the loading screen.  
See [Requirements](https://www.8thwall.com/docs/quickstart/legacy/getting-started/requirements/) for more information.

---

### Are there view limits on the free workspace?
No view limits. All experiences have unlimited views.

---

### What are the differences between my existing Pro Plan and the free workspace?
the free workspace does **not** support:

- Custom invoicing  
- App key access for off-platform development  
- Connecting custom domains  
- Publishing projects publicly  

---

### What do I do if I need to update my billing information after I downgrade?
To update your billing information, please refer to this section of our documentation:  
[Update Billing Information](https://www.8thwall.com/docs/account/settings/billing-information/)

---

### Can I have my billing set to invoicing for my new 8th Wall Account if I downgrade?
No, we require all payments to be made by **credit card** unless you have a pre-existing agreement with us.

---

### How do I cancel my account?
To cancel your account, go to the Accounts page and click **“Manage Plan”**.  
See our documentation:  
[Cancel Plan](https://www.8thwall.com/docs/legacy/guides/account-settings/#cancel-plan)
