## A-Frame: Shared AR + VPS Bespoke

This template combines the [Shared AR Lobby Template](https://www.8thwall.com/8thwall/empty-shared-lobby)
and the [Lightship VPS Bespoke Template](https://www.8thwall.com/8thwall/empty-vps) to demonstrate
Shared AR Colocation.

The cube is placed at the origin of the VPS Wayspot mesh, and it is placed consistently on all
player sessions.

**Note**: Shared AR is supported on `Plus`, `Pro`, and `Enterprise` plans. It is **not supported**
for `Starter` plans.
